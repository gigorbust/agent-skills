# Content Validator

**Purpose**: Validate MezTal content against quality and compliance standards

## Validation Rules

### Required Elements

1. **Cost Savings Language**
   - Must include: "approximately 40% cost savings"
   - Must be accurate and not exaggerated

2. **Location References**
   - Must mention both: Guadalajara AND Mexico City
   - Use proper capitalization
   - Context should be relevant

3. **SEO Requirements**
   - Title: 50-60 characters
   - Meta description: 150-160 characters
   - H1: One per page, includes primary keyword
   - Internal links: Minimum 3-5 per page

### Forbidden Elements

1. **Salary Figures**
   - No specific dollar amounts
   - No salary ranges
   - No compensation details

2. **Timeline Promises**
   - No "in X days" promises
   - No "within X weeks" guarantees
   - No specific delivery dates

3. **Competitor Mentions**
   - No mentions of: Toptal, Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer
   - No direct comparisons

4. **Unsubstantiated Claims**
   - No guarantees without evidence
   - No absolute statements ("always", "never", "guaranteed")

### Quality Standards

1. **Content Length**
   - Pillar pages: 4,000-5,000 words
   - Service pages: 2,000-3,000 words
   - Blog posts: 1,500-2,500 words

2. **Structure**
   - Clear H2 hierarchy
   - Question-based H2s for SEO
   - Proper internal linking
   - Schema markup (JSON-LD)

3. **Tone**
   - Professional but approachable
   - B2B focused
   - Value-driven
   - No hard sales language

## Validation Checklist

- [ ] Cost savings language present
- [ ] Both locations mentioned
- [ ] No salary figures
- [ ] No timeline promises
- [ ] No competitor mentions
- [ ] SEO metadata complete
- [ ] Internal links present
- [ ] Schema markup included
- [ ] Content length appropriate
- [ ] H2 structure logical
- [ ] Tone appropriate

## Usage

When validating content:

1. Check all required elements
2. Scan for forbidden elements
3. Verify quality standards
4. Provide specific feedback
5. Suggest improvements
