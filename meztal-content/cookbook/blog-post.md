# Blog Post Cookbook

## Specifications
- **Word Count**: 1,000-1,500 words
- **Purpose**: Thought leadership, timely topics, SEO long-tail
- **Internal Links**: 2-4 minimum (to relevant services/pillars)

## Structure Template

```markdown
---
title: [Compelling Title with Keyword] | MezTal Blog
description: [150-160 char meta description]
keywords: [primary, secondary, tertiary]
slug: /blog/[post-slug]/
type: blog
category: [Operations|Hiring|Industry|Best Practices]
word_count: [actual]
---

# [H1: Engaging Title]

[Opening hook: Question, statistic, or scenario that draws reader in]

## The Challenge
[2 paragraphs: Problem statement, why it matters now, who faces this]

## Key Insight / Main Argument
[Core thesis of the post]

## [Main Section 1]
[Supporting points with examples]

## [Main Section 2]
[Supporting points with examples]

## [Optional: Main Section 3]
[If needed for depth]

## Practical Takeaways
1. [Actionable item 1]
2. [Actionable item 2]
3. [Actionable item 3]

## How MezTal Helps
[Brief positioning: 1-2 paragraphs max]
[Mention both Guadalajara AND Mexico City if relevant]

## Conclusion
[Wrap-up, reinforce main point, soft CTA]

## Related Reading
- [Internal link 1]
- [Internal link 2]
```

## Blog Best Practices
- Lead with value, not sales pitch
- Use subheadings every 200-300 words
- Include at least one data point or example
- Keep MezTal mention brief (this is thought leadership, not sales)
- Conversational but professional tone

## Categories
- **Operations**: Process improvement, efficiency
- **Hiring**: Recruitment, team building, talent
- **Industry**: Senior Living, Healthcare, Technology, Finance
- **Best Practices**: How-to guides, tips

## Compliance Reminders
- ✅ "approximately 40% cost savings" (if cost mentioned)
- ✅ Guadalajara AND Mexico City (if locations mentioned)
- ❌ No salary figures
- ❌ No timeline promises
- ❌ No competitor names
