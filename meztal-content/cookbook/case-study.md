## Case Study Best Practices
- Use generic client descriptions unless permission granted
- Quantify results with percentages, not dollar amounts
- Follow Challenge → Solution → Results arc
- Include timeline of engagement (phases, not dates)
- Make it relatable to similar prospects

## Compliance Reminders
- ✅ "approximately 40% cost savings" (for cost results)
- ✅ Guadalajara AND Mexico City (if both involved)
- ❌ No real client names without permission
- ❌ No salary figures
- ❌ No competitor names
- ❌ No specific contract values
