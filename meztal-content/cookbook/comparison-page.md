## Comparison Best Practices
- Stay neutral in tone, let facts guide
- Use comparison table early for skimmers
- Address both options' strengths
- End with "it depends" + how MezTal helps decide

## Compliance Reminders
- ✅ "approximately 40% cost savings" (not exact %)
- ✅ Guadalajara AND Mexico City
- ❌ No salary figures in comparison
- ❌ No timeline promises
- ❌ NO competitor names (Toptal, Upwork, etc.)
