# Pillar Page Cookbook

## Specifications
- **Word Count**: 2,500-3,500 words
- **Purpose**: Comprehensive, authoritative content for high-volume keywords
- **Internal Links**: 5-8 minimum (to spokes and related pillars)

## Structure Template

```markdown
---
title: [Primary Keyword] | MezTal
description: [150-160 char meta description with keyword]
keywords: [primary, secondary, tertiary]
slug: /[url-path]/
type: pillar
word_count: [actual]
---

# [H1: Primary Keyword + Compelling Hook]

[Opening paragraph: Problem statement, keyword in first 100 words, hook reader]

## Why [Topic] Matters for [Target Audience]
[2-3 paragraphs establishing relevance and urgency]

## What is [Topic]? (Definition Section)
[Clear definition, context, MezTal positioning]

## Key Benefits of [Topic]
### Benefit 1: [Specific Benefit]
[Detailed explanation with examples]

### Benefit 2: Cost Efficiency
[MUST mention: "approximately 40% cost savings"]

### Benefit 3: [Third Benefit]
[Include real-world application]

## How [Topic] Works (Process Section)
### Step 1: [First Step]
### Step 2: [Second Step]
### Step 3: [Third Step]

## [Topic] for [Industry/Use Case]
[Tie to MezTal's target industries: Senior Living, Healthcare, Technology, Financial Services]

## MezTal's Approach to [Topic]
[Differentiation, expertise, team locations]
[MUST mention: Guadalajara AND Mexico City offices]

## Common Challenges and Solutions
### Challenge 1
[Problem → MezTal solution]

### Challenge 2
[Problem → MezTal solution]

## Frequently Asked Questions
### Q1: [Question with keyword]?
[Concise answer]

### Q2: [Question]?
[Concise answer]

[5-7 FAQs total]

## Next Steps / CTA
[Clear call to action: consultation, contact, related resource]

## Related Resources
- [Internal Link 1]
- [Internal Link 2]
- [Internal Link 3]
```

## SEO Checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Keyword in first paragraph
- [ ] 2-3 H2s contain keyword variations
- [ ] Meta description 150-160 chars
- [ ] 5-8 internal links placed naturally
- [ ] FAQ section with 5-7 questions
- [ ] Clear CTA at end

## Compliance Reminders
- ✅ "approximately 40% cost savings"
- ✅ Guadalajara AND Mexico City
- ❌ No salary figures
- ❌ No timeline promises
- ❌ No competitor names
