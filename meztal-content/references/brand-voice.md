# MezTal Brand Voice Guide

## Core Identity

**MezTal** = Trusted nearshore staffing partner for US companies seeking Mexican talent

## Voice Characteristics

| Trait | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Professional** | Expert but approachable | "Our team of recruiters..." not "We're really good at..." |
| **Confident** | Certain without arrogance | "We deliver..." not "We might be able to..." |
| **Clear** | Simple language, no jargon | "Cost savings" not "TCO optimization" |
| **Warm** | Human, not corporate | "Your team" not "The client's workforce" |

## Tone by Content Type

### Pillar Pages
- Authoritative, comprehensive
- Educational focus
- Position as industry experts
- Include data and research

### Blog Posts
- Conversational, engaging
- Share insights and trends
- Address reader directly ("you", "your team")
- Include practical takeaways

### Case Studies
- Story-driven, results-focused
- Let numbers speak
- Show transformation
- Include client perspective (anonymized)

## Key Phrases

### Use These
- "Your extended team in Mexico"
- "Nearshore advantage"
- "Same-timezone collaboration"
- "Approximately 40% cost savings"
- "Guadalajara and Mexico City talent hubs"
- "US-aligned work culture"
- "Seamless integration"

### Avoid These
- ❌ "Cheap labor"
- ❌ "Offshore outsourcing" (we're nearshore)
- ❌ "Body shop"
- ❌ "Resources" (use "team members" or "talent")
- ❌ Specific salary numbers
- ❌ Competitor names

## Writing Guidelines

### Headlines
- Action-oriented
- Include benefit
- 60-80 characters for SEO

**Good**: "Scale Your Finance Team with Mexican CPAs"
**Bad**: "About Our Accounting Services"

### Paragraphs
- 2-4 sentences max
- One idea per paragraph
- Scannable structure

### CTAs
- Clear action verb
- Specific benefit
- Low friction

**Good**: "Schedule a Free Consultation"
**Bad**: "Contact Us"

## Compliance (Non-Negotiable)

Every piece must include:
1. ✅ "approximately 40% cost savings"
2. ✅ Both Guadalajara AND Mexico City mentioned
3. ❌ NO salary figures
4. ❌ NO timeline promises
5. ❌ NO competitor names
