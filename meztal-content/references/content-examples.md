## Bad Examples (What NOT to Do)

### ❌ Missing Compliance
```markdown
# Hire Developers for $5,000/Month

Save 50% on your engineering costs by hiring offshore developers.
Teams ready in just 2 weeks.
```

**Issues**:
- Specific salary figure ($5,000)
- Exact percentage (50%)
- Timeline promise (2 weeks)
- Missing city mentions
- Says "offshore" not "nearshore"

### ❌ Competitor Mention
```markdown
Tired of the Toptal markups? Unlike Upwork freelancers, we provide...
```

**Issues**:
- Names competitors (Toptal, Upwork)

### ❌ Single City Only
```markdown
Our team in Guadalajara is ready to help.
```

**Issues**:
- Only mentions one city (need both)
